ZenRoad app is to demonstrate the capabilities of Damoov Telematics platform.
All features and services of this app are available via telematics SDK, API services as well as other pro data export options, and can be easily used for any other apps.
Also, ZenRoad is open-code and available for customization at no cost both for iOS and Android. With this, you can launch your ZenRoad-like app with a powerful telematics engine almost instantly.

How to use the app
The app tracks your trips automatically when you start driving. You don't need to keep the app open or press any buttons. Just start driving. All your trips with statistics and other processed data will be available in the app and our web-interface (sign up at damoov.com to get the access to it) after several minutes after your ride.
Permissions
To make telematics tracking work, please make sure you correctly granted all required permissions. Just follow the permissions wizard in the app. If you need any assistance, feel free to use the Permissions Guides below or ask for help via support live-chat at damoov.com (right-bottom of the screen) or right in the app chat.
